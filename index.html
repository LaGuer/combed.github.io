jupy-smartbuilding-amis Smart Building Metering Energy Dataset

Deployment

Example of deployment spanning across IIITD campus with over 200 smart meters recording various power related parameters every 30 s. The figure above shows the metering hierarchy.

About

Of all the sense points shown in the deployment above, jupy-smartbuilding-amis contains a month of smart meter data collected from different sensing points in IIITD's academic building.

jupy-smartbuilding-amis contains an energy related data set from an academic smart building where data is sampled more than once a minute. The data set comes with a loader allowing it to be easily plugged into nilmtk.

The entire project entire analysis scripts is open source and accessible on Github.

Details about deployment

We use Schneider electric based smart meters to collect data every 30 seconds. Our data collection system consists of RaspberryPi based controller and sMAP for data visualization and archiving.
